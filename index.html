Old Cat's Alamanac

Dry food is seen as an acceptable diet for house cats. However, if you look at the ingredients, the vast majority is filler. These fillers include brown rice, starches, and vegetables. Cats are obligate carnivores, meaning that they need a diet comprised of mostly meat.
